About the AI Career Coach

This coach is designed to be your unbiased, 2025-ready career guide. Its primary purpose is to help you analyze your existing profile (resume) or your target market (job role) and provide actionable, honest feedback. We focus on bridging the gap between your current skills and the fast-evolving demands of the Indian tech job market, particularly emphasizing AI/Automation resilience.

A Vibe Coding Project

This is a personal project, built with the intention of providing genuine, community-focused career advice (Vibe Coding).

It is particularly suited for:

  • Students & Freshers: Getting a realistic check on their resume against modern ATS systems.
  • Early Career Professionals: Identifying high-growth skills and planning realistic career path transitions.
